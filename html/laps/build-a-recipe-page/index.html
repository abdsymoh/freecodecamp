Lemon Poppy Seed Mini Cupcakes

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making Lemon Poppy Seed Mini Cupcakes! These small treats are bursting with refreshing lemon flavor and the unique texture of poppy seeds, making them perfect for parties or as a cheerful snack. Follow this simple recipe to create light, moist, and delicious cupcakes that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup milk (room temperature)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest (zest of one lemon)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350∘F (175∘C) and line a mini muffin pan (12 cavities) with small paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and poppy seeds.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter and sugar until light and creamy. Add the egg, lemon zest, and lemon juice and beat until combined.
  4. Gradually add half of the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, then add the milk, then add the remaining dry ingredients. Mix gently until just combined (do not overmix).
  5. Fill the cupcake liners about ⅔ full with batter.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let them cool completely before frosting or serving!
